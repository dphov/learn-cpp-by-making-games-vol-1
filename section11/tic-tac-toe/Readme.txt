For compilation use command:
cmake . && make

For running use command:
./tic-tac-toe

Usage info:
Game contains 2 player mode only. No AI, sorry.
First player is always "O", second is "X".
To put your character you have to enter the position of it, on the board.
For example (without quotes): "A1"
To quit the game, write command:
quit

Enjoy, and don't forget to have fun.

Additional info:
Your game can contain a bug
You will understand it if you will find unreadable symbol on position B3, sorry my bad. 
To avoid it, just rerun the game.
Good luck.
